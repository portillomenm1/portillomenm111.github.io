Drone Technology in Agriculture Croptracker blog This blog talks about drones in agriculture and how their usage has increased due to its different capacities leading to more efficiency in farming. Some of the functions and benefits it provides is by monitoring plant health and field conditions. Other types of drones can perform human tasks such as planting, seeding and spraying on fields. Although it creates a easier lifestyle for farmer due to its abilities; some imporvements need to be taken for big farms as it requires more precision.
KRAY The Most Efficient Industrial Crop-Spraying Drone KRAY Technology company The KRAY company gives great insight on the impact spraying drones have caused on farming. As the world are facing challenges such as climate change, bigger populations,poverty, pests,pathogens and land degragation; the spraying drone has helped in spraying fertilizers to crops with big speed as this could be impossible for human labor. This company provides farmers with a new solution to combat these challenges.
